\section{Repayment Terms}
\label{sec:repayment}

$\square$ \textbf{Lump-Sum Payment}
The \textbf{\textit{Borrower}} agrees to repay the full outstanding Loan Amount, including any accrued interest and fees, in one lump sum payment of \LendedAmountNumber{} \LendedAmountCurrencySymbol{}, due on or before \LoanLumpSumDue{}. Payment in full shall satisfy all obligations of the \textbf{\textit{Borrower}} under this Note.

\vspace{0.3cm}

\noindent $\square$ \textbf{Installment Payments}
The \textbf{\textit{Borrower}} agrees to repay the outstanding Loan Amount in \LoanInstallmentPeriod{} installments of \LoanInstallmentAmount{}  each, commencing on \LoanInstallmentStart{}  and continuing until the Loan Amount, including any interest, is paid in full.

\vspace{0.3cm}

\noindent Payments shall be made via:

\vspace{0.3cm}

\noindent
\begin{tabular}{p{6cm} p{10cm}}
\textbf{$\square$ Cash} & \LoanRepaymentCashMethod{} \\ 
\textbf{$\square$ Bank Transfer} & \LoanRepaymentBankMethod{} \\ 
\textbf{$\square$ Check} & \LoanRepaymentCheckMethod{} \\ 
\textbf{$\square$ Other} & \LoanRepaymentOtherMethod{} \\ 
\end{tabular}
