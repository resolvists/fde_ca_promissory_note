\section{Late Payment / Default}
\label{sec:late}

If the \textbf{\textit{Borrower}} fails to make any payment under this Note on or before its due date, such payment shall be considered late. In the event of a late payment:

\begin{itemize}
  \item \textbf{Late Fee:} The \textbf{\textit{Borrower}} shall pay a late fee of \LoanLateFee{} or \LoanLateFeeInterest{} of the overdue amount, whichever is greater, for each payment that is late.
  \item \textbf{Interest on Late Payments:} Any overdue amount shall accrue additional interest at a rate of \LoanLateFeeInterest{} per annum (or the maximum rate permitted by law), calculated from the due date until the date payment is received in full.
  \item \textbf{Notice of Late Payment:} The \textbf{\textit{Lender}} may, but is not required to, provide written notice of the late payment to the \textbf{\textit{Borrower}}. Failure to provide such notice does not waive the \textbf{\textit{Lender}}’s rights under this clause or this Note.
  \item \textbf{Cumulative Rights:} The imposition of a late fee or additional interest shall not prevent the \textbf{\textit{Lender}} from exercising any other rights or remedies under this Note, including but not limited to the right to demand immediate repayment of the entire outstanding Loan Amount.
  \item \textbf{ Default Trigger:} Repeated late payments or failure to pay within \LoanRepaymentDeadline{} days after the due date may constitute a default under this Note, entitling the \textbf{\textit{Lender}} to exercise any remedies available under this Agreement or at law.
\end{itemize}

\noindent Failure to pay constitutes a default under this Note.
