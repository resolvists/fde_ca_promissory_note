\section{Prepayment}
\label{sec:prepayment}

The \textbf{\textit{Borrower}} may, at any time, voluntarily repay all or part of the outstanding Loan Amount before its scheduled due date without incurring any penalty or fee. Any prepayment shall be applied first to any accrued interest, then to the principal balance of the Loan. Partial prepayments shall not waive or alter the \textbf{\textit{Borrower}}’s obligation to continue making subsequent scheduled payments unless otherwise agreed in writing by the \textbf{\textit{Lender}}. Prepayments must be made in the same manner as regular payments unless the \textbf{\textit{Lender}} consents to an alternative method.

\vspace{0.3cm}

\noindent The \textbf{\textit{Borrower}} understands and agrees that making a prepayment will not relieve them of any obligations incurred prior to the prepayment, including accrued interest or other charges. The \textbf{\textit{Lender}} shall provide the \textbf{\textit{Borrower}} with an updated statement reflecting the remaining balance, any accrued interest, and any changes to the payment schedule resulting from the prepayment.
