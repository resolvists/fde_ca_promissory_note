\section{Interest}
\label{sec:interest}

$\square$ \textbf{No Interest}
This loan is interest-free.

\vspace{0.3cm}

\noindent $\square$ \textbf{With Interest}
The Loan Amount shall accrue interest at a rate of \LoanInterest{} per annum calculated on the outstanding principal balance, beginning on the date the Loan is disbursed and continuing until the Loan Amount is paid in full. Interest shall be calculated on a \LoanInterestBase{} basis and shall be payable in accordance with the repayment terms set forth in this Note.

\vspace{0.3 cm}

\noindent Unless otherwise stated, interest shall accrue daily based on a 365-day year and shall be included in each scheduled payment. In the event of a late or missed payment, interest shall continue to accrue on any unpaid principal and accrued interest until payment is received in full.

\vspace{0.3 cm}

\noindent The \textbf{\textit{Borrower}} acknowledges that the stated interest rate represents the full and agreed compensation to the \textbf{\textit{Lender}} for extending the loan. In no event shall the interest charged under this Agreement exceed the maximum rate permitted by applicable law, and any excess interest collected shall be applied toward the principal balance or refunded to the \textbf{\textit{Borrower}}, as required by law.
